Starting Guidelines

Weight Selection

For Beginners (New to Lifting): Start light to master the form. Use the empty barbell (45 lbs/20 kg) for most presses. For deadlifts, start around 95 lbs/40 kg, and for rows, 65 lbs/30 kg.

For Some Experience: Choose a weight you can lift for 10-12 clean reps with good form. Always start conservatively.

Progressive Overload

This is the key to getting stronger. Aim to gradually increase the demand on your muscles.

Add 5 lbs (2.5 kg) to upper body exercises every 2 weeks.

Add 10 lbs (4.5 kg) to lower body exercises every 2 weeks.

Always master the form before increasing the weight.
